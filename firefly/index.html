From Joss Whedon, the creator who brought you Buffy the Vampire Slayer and Angel, comes the most epic sci-fi show of the century. In this short-run saga, a band of galiant, but fiesty, bounty hunters travel the galaxy on perilous missions. Their objective is merely to scrape by, meagerly surviving to the next day, but somehow they always find trouble and adventure lurking at every corner. Follow this loyal crew as they triumph and fail in a place where most surrender to their basic insticts. Where Western collides with Sci-fi, where integrity coalesces with tenacity is Firefly!
